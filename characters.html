Characters

Peter Parker

Peter Parker is a high school student who gains superhuman abilities after being bitten by a genetically altered spider. He becomes the superhero known as Spider-Man.

Spider Man

Spiderman is the superhero identity of Peter Parker. Aiming to stop crime across New York is his mission. He has the ability to shoot webs from his wrists, allowing him to swing from building-to-building. Other abilities include increased strength and agility and a special sense which allows him to detect danger without having to see it.

Mary Jane Watson

Mary Jane Watson is a high school student and Peter Parker's love interest. She is a kind-hearted and caring person who is supportive of Peter and his struggles.

Aunt May

Aunt May is Peter Parker's elderly aunt who raised him after his parents died. She is a loving and nurturing figure in Peter's life.

Uncle Ben

Uncle Ben is Peter Parker's uncle who raised him after his parents died. He imparts the famous lesson to Peter that "With great power comes great responsibility."

Harry Osborn

Harrt Osborn is Peter's best friend since a young age. His father is the CEO of the well known technology brand, Oscorp.

Norman Osborn

Norman Osborn is the owner of Oscorp. He is a kind and supportive parent to Harry, but pushes him at times. He later gains superhuman strength after injecting himself with a serum and becomes the Green Goblin.

Green Goblin

The Green Goblin is the alter ego of Norman Osborn. He is a villain who terrorises New York City and Spider Man. He has superhuman strength.
